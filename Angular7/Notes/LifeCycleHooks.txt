ngOnChanges 		- called when an input or output binding value changes	- OnChanges

ngOnInit 		- after the first ngOnChanges				- OnInit

ngDoCheck 		- developer's custom change detection			- DoCheck

ngAfterContentInit 	- after component content initialized			- AfterContentInit

ngAfterContentChecked 	- after every check of component content		- AfterContentChecked

ngAfterViewInit 	- after component's view(s) are initialized		- AfterViewInit 

ngAfterViewChecked 	- after every check of a component's view(s)		- AfterViewChecked

ngOnDestroy 		- just before the directive is destroyed.		- OnDestroy
